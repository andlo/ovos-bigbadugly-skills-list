### _skill-ovos-parrot.OpenVoiceOS_  
## Description:  
Turn OpenVoiceOS into a parrot. Speak a phrase and listen to it repeated in OVOS's voice.

"Hey Mycroft, start parrot"
"hello"
hello
"what"
what
"who are you"
who are you
"Stop parrot"
